Who we are

Built from experience

The world moves fast. Organizations grow, people come and go, systems change. Security needs to keep up without becoming a bottleneck.

CloudFellows was founded on a familiar observation: many organizations invest in security but have no clear picture of what it delivers or where the vulnerabilities are. They move through a complex environment without a clear direction.

We provide that direction. Not by stopping everything, but by creating structure within the movement so change can happen safely and you can move forward with confidence.

We're a small, specialized team with deep knowledge of the Microsoft stack. Not a large firm with generalists, but consultants who work daily with the environments they advise on.

Our vision

Security is about coherence

Individual tools solve individual problems. But real control over security only emerges when identity, devices and data are set up and managed as one cohesive whole.

🔗
Coherence over tools
The strength of a security environment isn't in the number of tools, but in how they fit together. We set up that whole.
📖
Clarity as a starting point
If a manager doesn't understand why something is set up a certain way, things will likely go wrong at the first change. We make decisions transparent.
📈
Security as an enabler
Well-designed security doesn't have to slow you down. It gives you the space to grow and change safely, with confidence in the foundation.
