What we do

Our services

We help organizations set up security that's clear and manageable, from analysis to implementation and knowledge transfer.

Advisory & strategy

Know where you stand

Before you improve anything, you need to know what you're working with. We map your current situation and translate it into a clear direction.

🔍
Analysis
Security assessment
An independent analysis of your identity setup, endpoint configurations, and data security. You get a clear picture of risks, dependencies, and areas for improvement, without the technical noise.
🗺️
Strategy
Security architecture
Based on your risks and objectives, we design a clear architecture. Every decision is justified and tailored to your organization, so you always know why things are set up the way they are.
Implementation

Concrete setup that works

We set up the four pillars of a manageable security environment: identity and access, endpoint security and management, data protection, and tenant management.

🔑
Identity & Access Management
Access and lifecycle
Setup of Microsoft Entra ID, Conditional Access and MFA. We configure lifecycle processes for joiner/mover/leaver so access is always up to date. Through Global Secure Access we connect users and devices securely to applications and the internet based on Zero Trust, regardless of location.
🛡️
Endpoint Protection & Management
Endpoint security
Setup of Microsoft Defender for Endpoint for threat detection and response on endpoints. Combined with Microsoft Intune for consistent device configuration and compliance checks on Windows and macOS. Security and device management as one cohesive setup.
🔒
Data Protection
Data security
Setup of Microsoft Purview for data classification, labeling and protection of business data. We map where sensitive information lives, label it based on sensitivity, and configure DLP policies that prevent data from leaving the organization through unmanaged channels.
☁️
Microsoft 365 Setup & Governance
M365 Governance & Control
Setup and governance of your Microsoft 365 tenant based on your risks and objectives. We structure roles, security policies and compliance settings for Exchange Online, Teams and SharePoint so your environment is transparently, demonstrably and transferably configured.
Knowledge transfer

Knowledge that sticks after the project

A good setup only has value if it stays manageable. We make sure knowledge and control are anchored within your own organization.

📘
Documentation
Clear management document
We deliver clear documentation of the setup: why decisions were made, how management works, and what to do in common situations.
🤝
Handover
Tailored knowledge transfer
Guidance for your IT team or management partner so they can continue independently. Not dependent on external knowledge, in control from within your own organization.
