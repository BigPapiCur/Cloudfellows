What we do

Our services

We help organizations set up security that's clear and manageable, from analysis to implementation and knowledge transfer.

Many organizations have built their security step by step, without a clear direction. The result is an environment that is difficult to oversee, hard to manage, and vulnerable to change.

We bring structure to the areas that matter most: identity and access, endpoint security, data protection and Microsoft 365 governance. Not as isolated solutions, but as a cohesive setup that makes your organization demonstrably stronger.

Advisory & strategy

Know where you stand

Before you improve anything, you need to know what you're working with. We map your current situation and translate it into a clear direction.

🔍
Analysis
Security assessment
An independent analysis of your identity setup, endpoint configurations, and data security. You get a clear picture of risks, dependencies, and areas for improvement, without the technical noise.
🗺️
Strategy
Security architecture
Based on your risks and objectives, we design a clear architecture. Every decision is justified and tailored to your organization, so you always know why things are set up the way they are.
Implementation

Concrete setup that works

We set up the four pillars of a manageable security environment: identity and access, endpoint security and management, data protection, and tenant management.

🔑
Identity & Access Management
Access and lifecycle
Setup of Microsoft Entra ID, Conditional Access and MFA. We configure lifecycle processes for joiner/mover/leaver so access is always up to date. Through Global Secure Access we connect users and devices securely based on Zero Trust.
🛡️
Endpoint Protection & Management
Endpoint security
Setup of Microsoft Defender for Endpoint for threat detection and response. Combined with Microsoft Intune for consistent device configuration and compliance checks on Windows and macOS.
🔒
Data Protection
Data security
Setup of Microsoft Purview for data classification, labeling and protection of business data. We map where sensitive information lives, label it based on sensitivity, and configure DLP policies that prevent data from leaving through unmanaged channels.
☁️
Microsoft 365 Setup & Governance
M365 Governance & Control
Setup and governance of your Microsoft 365 tenant based on your risks and objectives. We structure roles, security policies and compliance settings for Exchange Online, Teams and SharePoint.
