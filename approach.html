How we work

Our approach

No standard advisory report that ends up in a drawer. We work pragmatically: from analysis to a setup your team can manage on their own.

The process

Four steps to manageable security

We always start from your situation, not a predetermined toolkit. Each step builds on the previous one.

01
Analysis
We map your current setup: identity, endpoints, data and management processes. No assumptions, based on what's actually there.
02
Structure
Based on your risks and goals, we establish frameworks. What needs to be in order? What's the priority? We make decisions explicit.
03
Implementation
We set up the environment hands-on, together with your team. Configurations are documented as they're built.
04
Knowledge transfer
We wrap up with handover and documentation. Your team can continue independently. Management is no longer dependent on external knowledge.
